Hoops Analysis

Project Goals

The greatest basketball players in the world play on NBA teams, yet inspite of the tremendous athleticism and ability of these individual players, the teams comprised of these players have varying levels of success. The goal of "Hoops Analysis" is to is to analyze four statistical categories and determine if there is a correlation between the analyzed statistics and a team's win percentage.

Methodology

After assembling the dataset, Matplotlib will be used to plot various statistical differences between the winning teams, and the losing teams. Factors analyzed are "average point differential", "turnovers committed", "three-point percentage", and "overall field-goal percentage". These statistics will be plotted versus team win percentage.This site provides the source-data and visualizations created as part of the analysis, as well as explanations and descriptions of trends and correlations observed.
