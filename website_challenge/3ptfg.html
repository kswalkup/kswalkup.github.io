Plots - 3PT Field-goal Percentage

While a timely three-point shot may engergize a crowd and breathe fresh life into a team struggling to win a game, efficiency in shooting the three does not necessarily correlate into winning basketball games. The best teams do shoot three-pointers more efficiently that the worst, but the data illustrates that the correlation between three-point shooting efficiency and winning percentage is nearly non-existent.
